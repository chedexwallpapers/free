Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download wallpapers?

To download a wallpaper, simply click on the download button on the wallpaper you like. You'll be prompted to watch a short video and enter the required passwords. After completing these steps, the download will start automatically.

Why do I need to watch a video to download?

The video requirement helps support our platform and allows us to continue providing free, high-quality wallpapers. It's a small step that makes a big difference in keeping CheDex Wallpapers running.

Where do I find the passwords?

The passwords are shown in the video you need to watch before downloading. Pay close attention to the video as it will display the English, Filipino, and Bisaya passwords you need to enter.

Are the wallpapers really free?

Yes! All wallpapers on CheDex Wallpapers are completely free to download. We may show ads or require you to watch a video to support our platform, but there are no hidden fees or subscriptions.

Can I use these wallpapers commercially?

Our wallpapers are for personal use only. If you'd like to use them for commercial purposes (business websites, products, etc.), please contact us for licensing information.

How often do you add new wallpapers?

We add new wallpapers weekly. Follow us on social media to get updates when new collections are released!

What devices are these wallpapers compatible with?

Our wallpapers are optimized for most modern smartphones, tablets, and computers. Each wallpaper's resolution is displayed so you can choose the best fit for your device.

How can I submit my own wallpapers?

We love featuring community creations! Please email us at submissions@chedexwallpapers.com with your wallpaper files and information about yourself. If selected, we'll credit you when your wallpaper is published.

Still have questions?

Contact us at support@chedexwallpapers.com and we'll be happy to help!
